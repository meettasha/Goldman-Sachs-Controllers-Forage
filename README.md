# Goldman-Sachs-Controllers-Forage

## Overview

This repository showcases my work completed during the **Goldman Sachs Controllers Virtual Experience Program** on Forage. The simulation introduced the responsibilities of the Controllers division, including financial reporting, reconciliation, internal controls, and risk management.

## Objectives

* Understand financial reporting processes.
* Perform reconciliation and financial analysis.
* Identify operational and reporting risks.
* Learn the importance of strong internal controls.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Financial Analysis
* Financial Reporting
* Reconciliation
* Risk Assessment
* Internal Controls
* Analytical Thinking

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Microsoft Office
* Financial Reporting Techniques

## Project Workflow

1. Reviewed business and financial scenarios.
2. Examined financial information and reporting processes.
3. Identified discrepancies and operational risks.
4. Applied reconciliation techniques.
5. Documented observations and recommendations.


## Key Outcomes

* Gained practical exposure to financial control functions.
* Improved understanding of financial reporting frameworks.
* Learned the importance of reconciliation and risk identification.
* Developed problem-solving skills in financial operations.
